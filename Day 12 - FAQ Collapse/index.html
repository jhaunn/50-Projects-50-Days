Frequently Asked Questions

What accounts can I use to login to genshin Impact?

Depending on your device, you can login using miHoYo Account, Facebook, Twitter, Apple ID, Game Center, and PSN Account

Can I choose the game server?

Travelers playing on PC, iOS, and Android can choose their server region from the log-in screen. On PS4, the server region is set automatically based on your PSN Account's region.

Can I change my server and transfer my character from another server?

Travelers playing on PC, iOS, and Android can change servers. However, it will not be possible to transfer characters.

Is account data shared across different platforms?

Account data is shared across PC, iOS, and Android, so Travelers can log in from different platforms and continue their adventure. Due to PSN Account's restrictions, Travelers cannot use their PSN Accounts to log into the game on different platforms.

Can Travelers across different platforms play together in Co-Op Mode?

Once Travelers have reached the required Adventure Rank and completed related quests, they can play together with friends and participate together in co-op events. PS4 players can also join forces with friends from different platforms and embark on a variety of co-op adventures together.
